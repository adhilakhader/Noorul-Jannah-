ABOUT US

At Noor-ul-Jannah Online Academy, we believe that Qur’an education is more than just recitation. It is about nurturing hearts, building strong character, and connecting with Allah ﷻ in a meaningful way. Our mission is to make authentic and accessible Islamic learning available to every home. With the guidance of qualified teachers, we provide a nurturing space for children and adults alike to learn the Qur’an with Tajweed, strengthen their Hifz, and deepen their understanding of Islamic studies.
